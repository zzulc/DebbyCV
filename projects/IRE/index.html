Image Restoration and Enhancement


Introduction

This research project is mainly focusing on various applications of image resolution and enhancement, including image super-resolution, image denoising, image dehazing, image deblurring, and image enhancement. All of these works obtain competitive results. Until now, we have published many related papers on the flagship journals and conferences, e.g., IEEE TIP, TNNLS, IEEE TMM, IEEE TCSVT, ECCV, ICCV, ACMMM, IJCAI, etc. Our ultimate goal is to build a general image restoration model that does not need to be trained separately for different tasks. If you are interested in these works, welcome to join us.
