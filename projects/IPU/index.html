Image Perception and Understanding


Introduction

This research project is mainly focusing on various applications of image perception and understanding, including image classification, target detection, image segmentation, image stitching, and crowd counting. This is a new project and the focus of our research at this stage. Recently, we have published some related papers on the flagship journals and conferences, e.g., IEEE TMM, Pattern Recognition, etc. Our ultimate goal is to explore more high-level computer vision tasks to better understand images. If you are interested in these works, welcome to join us.
